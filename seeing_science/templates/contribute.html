How to contribute

Interested in contributing to Seeing Science?

That's great! The idea behind Seeing Science is a community-driven collaboration.

To contribute, head on over to github.com/delaneydunne/seeing-science. Given the spirit of the project is open source collaboration, contributing is as simple as making a Pull Request! Simply fork the repo, add your assets to seeing_science/static/your_name_here and you're done! To follow an example, please click here.
